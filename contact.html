Prayer Bulletin Board

Prayer for Rocco The Baby "Boofy"

Pray for Rocco The Baby "Boofy," a beloved Chihuahua pet of our congregant Fernando Quiroz. Boofy has been with our family since Obama's second term in office. Now over 18 years old, he can no longer see nor walk and is in constant pain, barking constantly for attention. Despite our efforts to comfort him, he remains irritated and in pain, sometimes hurting himself by slamming his head on the ground trying to get up. So we have to pay close attention to him to prevent him from hurting himself. It's a constant job being a caretaker for him, the struggle seems to be non-stop for him since he's barking constantly from 1:00 AM - 8:00 AM, takes a break then continues to cry in pain. We are debating whether to put him down, but we just can't see ourselves doing that, although we really have no recourse for him other than to hope his pain subsides. We want to make Boofy as comfortable as possible in his final days, we pray that the pain he's in can go away through these thoughts we petition the lord through prayer, we ask for guidance and relief for our beloved family friend and long time companion Rocco the Baby Boofy Monster. Hear our prayers lord and give our friend some reprieve from the pain, Amen and thank you Lord Jesus Christ
