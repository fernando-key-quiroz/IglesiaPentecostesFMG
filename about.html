Our History & Vision

Iglesia Pentecostés FMG first opened its doors in 1990 with a simple yet bold purpose: to serve Chicago’s neighborhoods both spiritually and practically. From our earliest days, we’ve aimed to be more than just another congregation—we want to be a living, breathing part of our city’s heartbeat, offering hope, healing, and hands-on help wherever it’s needed.

We began with a small group of dedicated believers who carried a vision of community transformation through love and faith.

Over the years, our history has been marked by moments of divine intervention and community outreach. The image to the right captures one such transformative moment in our journey. We believe in the Trinity, the authority of the Scriptures, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our growth and commitment keep our spirit lively and purposeful in serving our neighbors. Looking ahead, our vision remains clear: a community of believers lighting up Chicago with the power of the gospel.
